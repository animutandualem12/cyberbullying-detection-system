About System

The Cyberbullying Detection System is an AI-powered platform designed to identify and prevent online harassment across digital platforms. It analyzes text messages in real time using natural language processing (NLP) and machine learning models to detect toxic behavior, hate speech, and offensive language. The system helps educational institutions, organizations, and social platforms maintain a safer online environment by flagging harmful content and promoting positive digital communication.

With advanced analytics, administrators can review incidents, generate reports, and apply corrective measures to protect users from cyberbullying threats. Our mission is to empower communities to build a culture of respect, awareness, and online safety.
