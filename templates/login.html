About System

The Cyberbullying Detection System is an AI-powered platform designed to identify and prevent online harassment across digital platforms. It analyzes text messages in real time using NLP and machine learning to detect toxic behavior, hate speech, and offensive language. The system helps organizations and communities maintain safer online spaces by automatically flagging harmful content and encouraging respectful communication.

Administrators can track incidents, generate analytical reports, and respond to cyberbullying cases efficiently. Our mission is to strengthen online safety and awareness for a resilient digital society.
